    • Welcome to Exhibitions India Group

    Established in 1987, the Exhibitions India Group (EIG) is a leading trade promotion organisation that acts as an interface between government, industry, media, academia, etc. The Group creates opportunities for trade promotion, networking, encouraging investments, joint ventures and technology transfers through the platform of international exhibitions and conferences.

    EIG has organised over 100 international trade shows since inception, including the creation of some of the longest running and most impactful exhibitions in India. Today, EIG organises the largest technology and infrastructure expo in the country – Convergence India (including Internet of Things, Mobile India, EmbeddedTech, Fintech & Digital Gaming) and Smart Cities India (including Buildings, CleanTech, Smart Energy, Transport & Water). The Convergence India expo is at the forefront of India’s digital revolution, while the Smart Cities India expo is a reflection of India’s emerging modernization and development landscape.

    Exhibitions India Group comprises of five strategic business units dealing in exhibitions & conferences, international sales representation, publishing, stand design & fabrication and digital marketing solutions.

    Mission

    To maintain leadership through honest and transparent practices by providing world-class services to our customers; to develop their businesses worldwide through exhibitions, conferences, trade missions, market-entry strategies, etc.

    Vision

    To play an integral role in India’s growth, maintain leadership as a trade promotion organisation, and to set standards in excellence.

    Who We Are

    • EIG is headquartered in New Delhi, with branches in Bangalore, Chennai and Mumbai.

    • With 100 team members across India, and a database of over 400,000 Indian and overseas companies across several verticals, EIG has the ability to operate across the country and across industries.

    • EIG is the only trade fair organiser in India to have ISO 9001:2015 (for quality), and ISO 14001:2015 (for environment management system) certifications, as well as OHSAS 18001:2007 (for health & safety) certification.

    • EIG is also a member of UFI – The Global Association of Exhibitions Industry, which establishes and emphasises our commitment to quality.

    • We are the proud recipient of the ‘Outstanding Company to Promote Indo-US trade,’ awarded by the Indo-American Chamber of Commerce at the 11th Indo-US Economic Summit (2015).
